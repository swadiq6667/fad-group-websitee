üåü About FAD Group Arts & Sports Club, Perumbaipadi

Founded in 2013, The FAD Group Arts & Sports Club is a vibrant youth organization based in Paluvai, formed by the energetic and passionate boys of Perumbaipadi Street. What began as a small circle of friends driven by emotion, unity, and friendship has now grown into a strong community of 80+ dedicated members. The FAD Group is known for its social work, cultural programs, and sports activities, contributing positively to the development of the Paluvai area. Our members actively participate in community service, local events, and youth empowerment initiatives, standing together as a symbol of team spirit, creativity, and social responsibility.

üéØ Our Mission

Our mission is to create a platform where young minds can explore their passions, collaborate on meaningful projects, and develop skills in the fields of arts, media, Sports and social work. We strive to build a strong bond among the people of Paluvai, nurturing unity, creativity, and mutual support through every activity and initiative we take on.
